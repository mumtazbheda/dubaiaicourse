Refund Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

The short version

Show up live. If by the end of hour 1 of the workshop you don't love it, email us within that hour and we'll refund every dirham. Requests submitted after the first hour are not eligible.

1. Eligibility

You qualify for a 100% refund of the AED 499 ticket if and only if all of the following are true:

  • You attended the live workshop on Sunday, 16 May 2026 from 10:00 AM Dubai time.
  • You sent your refund request within 1 hour of the workshop start — i.e. before 11:00 AM Dubai time on the day of the workshop.

If your request reaches us after 11:00 AM Dubai time on Sunday, 16 May 2026, you are not eligible for a refund. No exceptions.

2. How to request a refund

Use any of the following before the deadline:

  • The Support form on the home page.
  • Reply to the order-confirmation email from Razorpay.
  • WhatsApp the number provided in your order-confirmation email.

Whichever channel you use, include the email address you used at checkout so we can match your registration.

3. Processing time

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 15 working days of approval. Bank settlement times may add a few extra days depending on your card issuer.

4. What happens to your access

When a refund is issued, your access to the following is revoked:

  • The 7-day workshop recording.
  • The 100-prompt CO-STAR vault.
  • The private WhatsApp community.
  • Any future office-hours sessions tied to this cohort.

This is a fair-both-ways policy: keep what you paid for, or get your money back, but not both.

5. Non-refundable scenarios

The following are not eligible for refund under any circumstances:

  • You did not attend the live workshop (replay-only access does not include refund rights).
  • You attended but did not request a refund within the first hour.
  • You disputed the charge with your bank without contacting us first — please email us before raising a chargeback.

6. Questions

If anything here is unclear, please reach out via the Support form before the workshop. We'd much rather answer your questions in advance than process a refund.
