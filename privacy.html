Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Solution Minds ("we", "us") collects and uses your personal information when you visit dubaiaicourse.vercel.app, register for the Get AI Ready workshop, or contact us via the support form.

1. What we collect

  • Checkout details — name, email and phone number you provide on the Razorpay payment page.
  • Account details — your email address when you sign in via the magic link we send after payment.
  • Support requests — name, email and message when you submit the support form.
  • Technical data — basic browser information (user-agent string) for debugging and abuse prevention.

2. How we use it

  • Deliver the live workshop, the recording, the prompt vault and the WhatsApp community.
  • Send your access link, Zoom link, reminders and post-workshop materials.
  • Process refunds where eligible (see our Refund Policy).
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Comply with UAE financial record-keeping obligations.

3. Third parties we share data with

  • Razorpay — payment processing.
  • Supabase — registration database and authentication (magic-link emails).
  • Vercel — website hosting.

Each of these providers operates under its own privacy policy. We do not sell your data to anyone.

4. Your rights

You can ask us to:

  • Confirm what data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your data, subject to legal record-keeping requirements (typically 7 years for financial records).
  • Stop sending you marketing emails (you can also unsubscribe directly from any such email).

To exercise any of these rights, use the support form on the home page.

5. Retention

We keep registration and payment records for 7 years to comply with UAE accounting rules. Workshop content access (recording, vault, WhatsApp) is provided for the duration described in the workshop offer. Support messages are kept until resolved plus a reasonable archive period.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Use the Support form on the home page.
